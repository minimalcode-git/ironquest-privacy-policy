Privacy Policy - IronQuest

Last updated: May 27, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the IronQuest application ("App", "Service", "we") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application.

By using IronQuest, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Exercise Data

  • Workout and exercise records
  • Weights, repetitions, and sets
  • Progress history
  • Personal workout notes

2.2 Device Data

No device data is collected or sent to external servers

2.3 Purchase Data

  • PRO subscription information
  • Transaction history (processed via RevenueCat)
  • Payment status

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 App Functionality

  • Store and display your workout data locally on your device
  • Calculate statistics and progress based on data you record
  • Allow you to manage and organize your workout routines

3.2 PRO Features

  • Verify subscription status
  • Enable premium features
  • Process payments via RevenueCat

4. Information Sharing

4.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We use third-party service providers to facilitate our Service:

  • RevenueCat: PRO subscription management
  • Google Play Store: App distribution and purchase processing

4.2 We Do Not Share

  • Your personal workout data is not sold or shared with third parties
  • We do not sell your personal information

5. Data Storage

5.1 Local Storage

  • Workout data is stored locally on your device
  • Settings and preferences remain in local storage

5.2 Security

  • We implement security measures to protect your data
  • Data is encrypted when appropriate

6. Your Rights

6.1 Access and Control

  • You can access all your data within the application
  • You can export or delete your data at any time

6.2 GDPR/CCPA Rights

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to deletion
  • Right to data portability

7. Data Retention

  • Workout data: Retained while you use the application
  • Purchase data: Retained as required by law
  • You can delete your data at any time

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes.

  • Through the application

11. Legal Compliance

This policy complies with:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
  • LGPD (Brazilian General Data Protection Law)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
  • Google Play Store Policies
